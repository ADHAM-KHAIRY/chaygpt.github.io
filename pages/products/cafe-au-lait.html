Cafe au Lait

Traditional French Cafe au Lait

$16

Experience the perfect balance of boldness and creaminess with our authentic Cafe au Lait. This classic French beverage combines our signature dark-roasted coffee with steamed milk in equal parts, creating a smooth, velvety texture that warms the soul. Each cup is prepared with care, using only premium coffee beans and fresh, locally-sourced milk.

What makes our Cafe au Lait special:

  • Premium dark-roasted coffee beans for rich flavor
  • Equal parts coffee and steamed milk for perfect balance
  • Velvety smooth texture with gentle foam
  • Served in traditional wide-bowl cups
  • Customizable with organic sweeteners

How We Prepare It

  1. We start with freshly ground premium dark-roasted coffee beans.
  2. The coffee is brewed using traditional French press method for full-bodied flavor.
  3. Fresh whole milk is gently steamed to create a silky microfoam.
  4. Equal parts coffee and steamed milk are combined in perfect harmony.
  5. The beverage is served in our signature wide-bowl cups to enhance the aroma experience.
  6. Optional organic sweeteners are available on request.

Ingredients

  • Premium dark-roasted coffee beans (Brazilian and Colombian blend)
  • Fresh locally-sourced whole milk
  • Filtered water
  • Optional: organic cane sugar, maple syrup, or honey
